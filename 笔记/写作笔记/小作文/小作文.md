#### 1.1 **第一大句型：**描述趋势（上升、下降、不变、波动）

1. `(There be)`**There** **was/is/will be** **a adj+ n. in the** **number/percentage of** **(A)（data）time period.** 

   > **有一个  变化 (in)什么的 在什么时候变了多少**

   <img src="./assets/image-20240909165141317.png" alt="image-20240909165141317" style="zoom:50%;" />

   

2. **The** **number/percentage** **of (A) v.+ adv. (data) time period.**

    > **什么的 变化 在什么时候变了多少**

    **The percentage of** elderly people increased sharply from 5% in 1990 to 21% in 2000

    The proportion of the olds rose rapidly from 5% in 1990 to 21% in 2000

   

3. **Time period** **witness/see** **a adj.+n. in the** **number/percentage** **of** **(A)（data）.**

   > **在什么时候  (witness)变化 (in)什么的 变了多少**

   （**Time period 通常为** the 5 years，the next 4 years，）

   `The period from 1994 to 1998` witnessed ...
   
   **The period between** 1990 and 2000 witnessed/ saw/ experienced a sharp increase in the percentage of elderly people from 5% to 21%
   
   



#### 1.2 第二大句型：描述最高值最低值（一个对象在一段时间之中的最高点和最低点）

（在一篇文章中，以下三个小句型最多各出现一次）

1. **It** **reached/reaches/will reach** **its** **highest point/peak/lowest** **point/bottom** **at (data) time.**（建议与上一个句型写成并列句）

   <img src="./assets/image-20240909165531628.png" alt="image-20240909165531628" style="zoom:50%;" />

   The period from 1994 to 1998 saw a fluctuation in the number of other reasons **and** **it reached its lowest point at 954 in 1995.**

   

   

2. **上升/下降：peaking/bottoming at data（time）**

3. **上升/下降：reaching its** **highest point/peak/lowest point/bottom** **at** **data（time）**

   The number of holiday increased sharply **until**(一直增长) 1998**, peaking at 29700 in 1998.**

   The number of holiday increased sharply until 1998**, reaching its highest point** **at 29700 in 1998**

#### 1.3 常用三大高级句式(动态图)

##### （1）第一大句型：分词结构

1. 电力供应差不多增长两倍（double），从 50 涨到 100。

   Electricity supply **nearly doubled** ，**rising** from 50 to100.

2. 开荒（land clearance）是上地退化（degradation）的主要原因，占据了 10%。

   Land clearance was the main reason for degradation ，**accounting for** 10%.

3. **英国数值的趋势预料会继续持续，在 2030 年几乎下降到91%。**

   The trend in ``the figure for`` Britain `is expected to` continue, **dropping** **to almost 91% in 2030.** 

4. **法国高质量水的比例被预测有一个升幅，在 2030 年到 96%**

   The proportion of high-quality water in France ``is projected to`` show an upward trend, **reaching 96% in 2030**

##### （2）第二大句型：定语从句

 **用 after which ，during which time ，where 等引导的定语从句**

1. 手机用户的数量在 2005 上升到 2 百万，在那之后数字保持平。

   The number of mobile phone users rose to 2 million in 2005 ，**after which** the figure **remained constant.**
   
1. **意大利高质量水的比例在 1980 年左右下降到 85%，之后在2030** **年预测上升到 96%**

   The proportion of high-quality water in Italy dropped to 85% in around 1980, **after which it will rise to 96% in 2030.** 

##### （3）第三大句型：状语从句

**用 while，或者 although 引导的状语从句，或者是 but 引导的并列句**

1. 结婚的数字上升，而离婚的数字下降。

   The number of marriages rose ，**while** the number of divorces dropped.

2. 虽然读科学的男生比女生要多，这个数字在下降。

   **Although** men outnumbered women in science ，this number declined.

3. **德国的数值可能在 2020 年上升到 92%，虽然它可能在 2030 年下降到 88%**

   The figure for Germany is likely to rise to 92% in 2020, **although** it will possibly decline to 88% in 2030. 

4. **当高质量的水的比例在英国下降时，法国的数值显示出一个相反的趋势。**

   The proportion of high-quality water in Britain declined, **`while` the figure for France showed the opposite trend.**
